The Idea

「知らせて.jp」 is a collaborative REPORT MANAGEMENT solution for CITIZENS and local GOVERNMENT:

  • Citizen Engagement
  • Collaboration
  • Optimized Workflows
  • Transparency and Visibility

Also positive reports!

  • Ideas and proposals
  • General tasks
  • Any kind of local information

=> Location-based Task Management

Workflow

[Step 1] Submit Report

  • With smartphone or from webbrowser
  • Attach photo and description
  • Provide additional information,
    ie. severity, classification, etc.

[Step 2] Review Report

  • Change report status
  • Assign report to group or person
  • Set additional attributes,
    ie. tags, start or end date, etc.
  • Provide feedback

[Step 3] Resolve Report

  • Change report status (in progress, resolved)
  • Document work (ie. photos, working time)
  • Provide feedback

[Step 4] Close Report

  • Change report status to "closed"
  • Provide feedback

=> Happy Citizen!

Open Government

Our goal is to IMPROVE the SERVICE of local governments:

  • Easy for citizens to submit report
  • Grow local volunteer experts
  • Minimize citizens frustration
  • Improve visibility of public services
  • Provide a maximum of transparency

Our goal is to OPTIMIZE the maintenance WORKFLOW:

  • Make use of the knowledge and motivation of citizens
  • Properly manage report assignments
  • Document report progress and status
  • Plan the daily work of your service staff
  • Keep the city infrastructure in good shape

Open Government

  • Listen to your citizens
